About the alterNet

Where retro tech meets futuristic fashion.

the alterNet is a curated space for nostalgic innovation. We refurbish early 2000s electronics and pair them with cyberpunk-inspired accessories to spark imagination and sustainability. Our aesthetic is rooted in Y2K futurism, glossy surfaces, translucent plastics, and glitchy optimism.

We believe the future isn’t new—it’s recycled and reimagined. Join us in building a future you can plug into, customize, and wear.
